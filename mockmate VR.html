How It Works

MockMate combines the power of virtual reality with advanced artificial intelligence to provide a fully immersive interview training experience. Users wear a VR headset to enter virtual environments that replicate real interview settings. They are guided through a variety of scenarios including technical interviews, HR screenings, and panel interviews.

During each session, MockMate records and analyzes your performance using voice recognition, body posture tracking, and behavioral analytics. It then provides feedback on aspects such as speech clarity, confidence, and response quality. You can track your progress over time and refine your approach for real-world success.
